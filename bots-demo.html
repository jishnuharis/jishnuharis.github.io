How it works

Users send /find to join a queue. The bot pairs two strangers instantly and relays every message bidirectionally — neither side ever sees the other's Telegram ID. Gender and country filters narrow the match pool. Admin controls for moderation are baked in.

Command system
▸
/find — match with a random stranger
▸
/next — skip to the next partner
▸
/stop — end the current chat
▸
/profile — view your coins, rating & stats
▸
/steal — coin-steal minigame with partner
▸
/rate /report — rate or flag your partner
▸
/gender /country — set match filters
